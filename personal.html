Personal

Family

I was raised my my Mexican parents and have two sisters. Growing up, my parents used to always take us to visit our family in Mexico since we were so close to the border in Arizona. It kept me closer to our culture and allows me to embrace it in every room I'm in. I am a first generation graduate who aspires to get more Latina women in tech.

Chapo the Bulldog

This is my family's bulldog, Chapo! For being a bulldog, he is very energetic and loves people as much as he loves food. His favorite pass times are sleeping and riding his beloved skateboard.

Travel

I had the opportunity to study abroad the summer of my junior year in college and it was one of the most incredible experiences I've had. Learning more about different cultures and seeing different parts of the world has sparked my desire to travel as much as I can in my lifetime. This photo was taken during my walk to class in Granada, Spain!

Food

Living in a Mexican household, I grew up eating many of the traditional dishes. Food is not only fuel, but apart of our culture. When I moved half way across the country from my family, cooking made me feel closer to them and was a way I express appreciation to my friends. I also enjoy trying different cuisines, one of my favorite being Asian cuisine!

Movement

One of my most important hobbies is exercise. Not only do I feel better after doing it, but it is also something that I enjoy learning more about. The summer of 2025, I trained and participated in my first triathalon! I enjoy to run, bike, and lift weights. I also trained to run the TCM 10 mile and have a goal to run a marathon one day!
